a little about me

Thank you for stopping by.

I was born in West India and then I grew up or so they say – but I’d like to believe I still have a long way to go.
During my Bachelor's, I was selected as one of the Young Innovators of India by the Prime Ministers' Office to represent the country at an International Hackathon and got felicitated by the Indian Prime Minister and the Education Minister of Singapore as my team finished as the runner’s up. This shaped my life in ways I can’t express enough.

I spend most of my free time planning my activities and writing about every little thing I can. On most days, you’ll find me diving deeper and deeper into the realms of Computer Science.
Although I not-so-secretly wish I was a writer of dark academia and could teleport to a world with vintage clothes, decorated walls and jazz music and lighted candles.
I do feel a strong connection with images. I have put some images close to my heart. I hope you like them too.

I am extremely passionate about learning new things and obsessively read 15+ books a year. I think I’m sarcastic and blunt. However, most people find me amicable. I hope you discover a different shade of me someday!
